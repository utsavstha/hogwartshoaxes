Dragon Pox Panic Grips Diagon Alley! Healers Scramble for Bezoars as New Strain Emerges!

April 6, 2025 | By Elphias Doge II

Diagon Alley is in a state of controlled chaos this morning after reports of a highly contagious, and frankly rather embarrassing, new strain of Dragon Pox have surfaced. Initial cases were identified amongst goblin-run banking employees at Gringotts, leading some to speculate on the involvement of dark magic, or perhaps just exceptionally unsanitary gold-counting practices.

The Ministry of Magic’s Department of Magical Accidents and Catastrophes, along with a hastily assembled team of Healers from St. Mungo’s, are working tirelessly to contain the outbreak. Head Healer Augusta Longbottom (née Bell) issued a statement earlier this morning urging calm, while simultaneously hoarding the entire St. Mungo’s bezoar supply. “There is no need for panic,” she declared, her eyes darting suspiciously around the press conference. “Simply avoid close contact with anyone exhibiting symptoms. And for Merlin’s sake, wash your hands after handling galleons!”

The new strain, dubbed ‘Poxzilla’ by mischievous Hogwarts students (a nickname Headmistress McGonagall reportedly finds ‘utterly inappropriate’), is characterized by exceptionally large, bright green pustules that reportedly emit a faint but persistent odor of dungbombs. One unfortunate victim, a Mr. Barnaby Crumplepot, owner of ‘Crumplepot’s Curious Curios,’ was overheard lamenting that his new ‘glow-in-the-dark’ pustules had actually increased foot traffic to his shop, though he admitted sales of Dreamless Sleep Potion had also skyrocketed.

The Daily Prophet has learned that Professor Severus Snape’s old potion recipes are being desperately scoured for a potential cure, though insiders suggest his notes are mostly filled with scathing criticisms of Harry Potter’s potion-making abilities. Minister for Magic Kingsley Shacklebolt has assured the public that the situation is under control, despite rumors of a potential bezoar black market emerging in Knockturn Alley. He has urged all witches and wizards to remain vigilant and to avoid unnecessary travel. Particularly to areas known for harboring dragons… or goblins with questionable hygiene habits.
