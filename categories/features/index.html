The Unseen Battlefield: Wizengamot Politics and the International Confederation of Wizards

March 29, 2025 By Bathilda Bagshot (Occasional Correspondent)

The wizarding world, often perceived as a realm of enchanted objects and fantastical creatures, is not immune to the complexities of geopolitics. While wands replace weapons of mass destruction (mostly), and owls substitute diplomatic cables, the fundamental principles of power, influence, and national interest remain firmly in place. This week, the Daily Prophet delves into the often-opaque world of wizarding international relations.

The International Confederation of Wizards (ICW), headquartered in Switzerland, is the primary governing body for the global wizarding community. Ostensibly designed to foster cooperation and prevent breaches of the Statute of Secrecy, the ICW has become a hotbed of political maneuvering. Albus Dumbledore’s long tenure as Supreme Mugwump, cut short by his untimely death, saw a period of relative stability, albeit one often criticized for its inherent British bias.
