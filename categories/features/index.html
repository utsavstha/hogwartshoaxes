While the Ministry of Magic strives for a harmonious co-existence (mostly), tensions are brewing beneath the surface, threatening to bubble over like a poorly brewed Polyjuice Potion.

The Ministry’s representative, Senior Undersecretary Dolores Umbridge (currently sporting a fetching shade of fuchsia and a disturbingly cheerful smile), maintains that everything is “perfectly delightful” and that any Goblin grievances are “simply…misunderstandings.” Critics, however, point to the undeniably high concentration of Goblin-related “misunderstandings” as evidence of systemic inequality.
